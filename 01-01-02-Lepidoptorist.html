Page 2.

Panel 1.

The butterfly spins, throwing Li Jing from its back.

Captions: Scientists back home are working on fixing that... While we raid American skies to slow the cause.

Panel 2.

Li Jing tumbles through the sky. With a flick of her wrist, a grappling hook is fired, grabbing hold of the butterly.

Caption: Every butterfly we kill buys us more time.

Panel 3.

Li Jing swings back towards the butterfly.

Captions: And of course bullets just bounce off their exoskeletons... So of course we have to fight close and personal.

Panel 4.

Li Jing lands on the butterfly, driving her scalpel through the back of the creature’s head.

Captions: Striking at weak-spots with our laser-scalpels.

Panel 5.

A slice of the scalpel sends the butterfly’s head flying through the air.

Captions: Damn. Fake head. It happens. Classic evolutionary adaptation.

Panel 6.

Li Jing is swinging through the air again, as the buterfly’s body opens up to reveal a new head.

Caption: Hiding the real one beneath an unfolding thorax is a more recent mutation.
