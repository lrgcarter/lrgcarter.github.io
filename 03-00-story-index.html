Shanks Family Curse

When Armitage watches his parents fall foul of an ancient family curse, it leaves him somewhat out of sorts. His new guardians are retired pirates, so their solution to every problem is high adventure on the seven seas. They’re soon dragging Armitage over the waves, to face whatever the ocean can throw at them. Armitage needs to hurry up and convince them that his depression calls for more than just fresh air and exercise. Otherwise it’s only a matter of time before the Shanks Family Curse strikes again...

What Urbi Did Next

The events of Shanks Family Curse left Urbi Hotep with a couple of unresolved issues.

Most pressingly, she still needs to vanquish the invaders that long ago conquered her home. The invaders that enslaved her people and fed them to carnivorous fish-things. The invaders that are nearing the end of their plans for total world domination.

What Urbi Did Next is a short follow-up tale to Shanks Family Curse.
