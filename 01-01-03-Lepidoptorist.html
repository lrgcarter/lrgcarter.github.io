Page 3.

Panel 1.

A flick of the other wrist actives Li Jing’s wrist mounted gas canisters.

Caption: Still, insecticide canisters remain a versatile weapon.

Panel 2.

The butterfly reels. Li Jing hovers for a second in the air, once again readying her scalpel.

Panel 3.

Li Jing dives, her scalpel cutting through the butterfly, separating its thorax from abdomen.

Panel 4.

Butterflies fall to pieces in the background. Li Jing and others from her team glide back towards their transport.

Captions: There’s only time to tackle a few targets on each raid. Then it’s back to our aircraft and home before we’re noticed.

Panel 5.

The aircraft blows up, incinerating Li Jing’s teammates.

Caption: Right... Scratch that plan.

Panel 6.

The culprit was an American jet, and it’s flying right at us!

Panel 7.

Li Jing charges towards the jet, her scalpel fully powered.

Caption: You’d think they might welcome our efforts to clean up their mess.

Panel 8.

Li Jing and the jet collide. The jet pilot shields themselves from shattered glass as Li Jing smashes through the windshield.

Caption: Instead of classifying us as hostile forces invading their skies.
